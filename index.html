Guillaume Caulier

I’m a webdesigner & front-end developer living in Lille

Creative and technical at the same time, I combine web design and front-end development to design digital products that are thoughtful, accessible and meeting the latest technical criteria.

Web design is more
than just a passion

Sensitive to webdesign, I experiment every day through wireframe design or animation design, always in a user experience logic. I keep an active watch on the latest artistic, UI & UX design trends, to designing outstanding digital products. My tools are sketch & invision and his plugin Craft.

A logical mind combined
with a creative spirit?

Front-end Development
is more than just code

My favorite technology is JavaScript, especially with TweenMax. Technology to which, of course, I couple HTML5 and CSS3 (Sass). I also develop with the automation of tasks, such as minimization, etc., by means of Gulp and proceeds to versioning via Git.

I think having a good knowledge and understanding of the development environment and front-end issues is vital for a webdesigner. And vice versa.
